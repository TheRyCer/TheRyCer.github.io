About the studio

A small studio building precise games.

RPD33 Studio is an independent game studio focused on compact tactical experiences — short to start, deep to master. Our work lives at the intersection of stealth, strategy, and clean systems design.

We make small, focused games that respect your time. Less filler. More decisions. Our debut, Ninjagrid, is a turn-based stealth puzzle now available on Android, with Windows and Web demos.
